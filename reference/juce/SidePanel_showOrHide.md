#### showOrHide()


 void SidePanel::showOrHide ( bool show ) 
 

Shows or hides the SidePanel.This will animate the SidePanel to either its full width or to be hidden on the left or righthand side of its parent component depending on the value of positionOnLeft that was passed to the constructor.Parameters

 show if true, this will show the SidePanel and if false the SidePanel will be hidden
