A reentrant mutex.A CriticalSection acts as a reentrant mutex object. The best way to lock and unlock one of these is by using RAII in the form of a local ScopedLock object have a look through the codebase for many examples of how to do this.In almost all cases you'll want to declare your CriticalSection as a member variable. Occasionally you may want to declare one as a static variable, but in that case the usual C++ static object orderofconstruction warnings should be heeded.See alsoScopedLock, ScopedTryLock, ScopedUnlock, SpinLock, ReadWriteLock, Thread, InterProcessLock
