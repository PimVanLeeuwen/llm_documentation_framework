An IIR filter that can perform low, high, or bandpass filtering on an audio signal, and which attempts to implement basic threadsafety.This class synchronises calls to some of its member functions, making it safe (although not necessarily realtimesafe) to reset the filter or apply new coefficients while the filter is processing on another thread. In most cases this style of internal locking should not be used, and you should attempt to provide threadsafety at a higher level in your program. If you can guarantee that calls to the filter will be synchronised externally, you could consider switching to SingleThreadedIIRFilter instead.See alsoSingleThreadedIIRFilter, IIRCoefficient, IIRFilterAudioSource
