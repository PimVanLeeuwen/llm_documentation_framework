Subclass of AudioFormatReader that reads samples from a group of playback regions.PlugIns typically use this to draw the output of a playback region in their UI.In order to read from playback regions, the reader requires an audio processor that acts as ARA playback renderer. Configuring the audio processor for realtime operation results in the reader being realtime capable too, unlike most other AudioFormatReaders. The reader instance will take care of adding all regions being read to the renderer and invoke its processBlock function in order to read the region samples.The reader becomes invalid ifany region properties are updated in a way that would affect its samples,any region content is updated in a way that would affect its samples, orany of its regions are destroyed.
