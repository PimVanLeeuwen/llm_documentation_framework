A component that displays an MPEcompatible keyboard, whose notes can be clicked on.This component will mimic a physical MPEcompatible keyboard, showing the current state of an MPEInstrument object. When the onscreen keys are clicked on, it will play these notes by calling the noteOn() and noteOff() methods of its MPEInstrument object. Moving the mouse will update the pitchbend and timbre dimensions of the MPEInstrument.See alsoMPEInstrument
